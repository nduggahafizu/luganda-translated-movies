About Luganda Movies

Uganda's premier platform for VJ translated movies, bringing international entertainment to Ugandan audiences in their native language.

Our Story

Luganda Movies was born from a simple idea: to make world-class entertainment accessible to Ugandan audiences in their native language. We recognized that language should never be a barrier to enjoying great movies and TV shows.

Founded in 2024, we've partnered with Uganda's most talented VJ (Video Jockey) translators who bring their unique style and cultural understanding to every translation. These VJs don't just translate‚Äîthey localize, adding context, humor, and cultural references that make international content feel authentically Ugandan.

Today, we're proud to be the leading platform for Luganda-translated entertainment, serving thousands of viewers across Uganda and the diaspora.

Our Mission

To bridge the entertainment gap by providing high-quality, culturally relevant Luganda translations of international movies and TV shows, while supporting and promoting Uganda's talented VJ community.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading platform for localized entertainment, expanding beyond Luganda to other African languages, and creating a sustainable ecosystem for content creators and translators.
