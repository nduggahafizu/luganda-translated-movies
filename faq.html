General Questions

What is Unruly Movies?

Unruly Movies is Uganda's premier platform for Luganda-translated movies featuring licensed Video Jockeys (VJs). We provide high-quality movie translations and commentary in Luganda, making international films accessible to Ugandan audiences.

What is a VJ (Video Jockey)?

A VJ (Video Jockey) is a licensed professional who provides Luganda translations and live commentary for movies. VJs are recognized cultural entertainers in Uganda and East Africa, adding local flavor and context to international films.

Is Unruly Movies free?

We offer both free and premium content. Basic access is free with ads. Premium subscriptions remove ads and provide access to exclusive content, early releases, and HD quality streaming.

Technical Support

We recommend at least 2 Mbps for SD quality and 5 Mbps for HD quality. Our player automatically adjusts quality based on your connection speed.

Can I watch on mobile devices?

Yes! Unruly Movies is fully responsive and works on all devices - smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. Just visit our website from any browser.
