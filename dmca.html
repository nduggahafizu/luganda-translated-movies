Our Commitment to Copyright

Unruly Movies is committed to respecting intellectual property rights. We take copyright infringement seriously and will promptly remove any content that violates copyright laws upon receiving valid notice.

Unruly Movies, operated by Nduga Hafizu, is a platform that showcases Luganda translations by licensed Video Jockeys (VJs). We operate in compliance with applicable copyright laws and the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

Understanding VJ Translations

Video Jockeying (VJ) is a recognized cultural practice in Uganda and East Africa where commentators provide live or recorded Luganda-language translations and cultural commentary for films. This practice:

  • Is a legitimate form of cultural expression in Uganda
  • Involves licensed VJs who are recognized professionals
  • Adds original creative value through translation and commentary
  • Serves educational and cultural purposes

We work with VJs who operate within legal frameworks and respect content creators' rights.

Filing a DMCA Takedown Notice

If you believe that content on our platform infringes your copyright, please send us a DMCA takedown notice with the following information:

  1. Your Contact Information: Full legal name, physical address, email address, and phone number
  2. Identification of Copyrighted Work: Description of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed
  3. Location of Infringing Content: Specific URL(s) where the allegedly infringing material is located
  4. Good Faith Statement: A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized
  5. Accuracy Statement: A statement that the information in the notice is accurate
  6. Authorization Statement: A statement that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner
  7. Signature: Your physical or electronic signature

Warning: Knowingly submitting false DMCA claims may result in legal liability. Please ensure your claim is valid before submitting.

Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was wrongly removed, you may file a counter-notification containing:

  1. Your physical or electronic signature
  2. Identification of the removed content and its former location
  3. A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the content was removed by mistake
  4. Your name, address, and phone number
  5. Consent to the jurisdiction of your local federal court
  6. A statement that you will accept service of process from the complainant

Our Response Process

Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice, we will:

  1. Acknowledge Receipt: Within 24 hours of receiving your notice
  2. Review the Claim: Evaluate the validity of the takedown request
  3. Take Action: Remove or disable access to the allegedly infringing content
  4. Notify the Uploader: Inform the content uploader of the takedown
  5. Document the Process: Keep records of all DMCA actions

We aim to respond to valid takedown requests within 48-72 hours.

Repeat Infringer Policy

In accordance with the DMCA, we maintain a policy of terminating, in appropriate circumstances, users who are repeat infringers of copyright.

  • First offense: Warning and content removal
  • Second offense: Temporary account suspension
  • Third offense: Permanent account termination
